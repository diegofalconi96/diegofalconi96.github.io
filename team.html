Meet Our Teachers

Fabiola Cando

Teacher


Fabiola Cando held her master’s degrees in Applied Linguistics to Bilingual Teaching: English-Spanish and Planning and Administration of Education from the Catholic University of Ecuador and the Technical University of Cotopaxi respectively. She got a Didactics of Higher Education diploma from the Technical University of Cotopaxi. She participated in an international exchange program—Language and Culture at Aalborg University. Also, she got scholarships from the U.S. government to get the TESOL Certificate and a course in Professional Development for Teacher Trainers. Her research interests focus on Language Education and Teachers’ Professional Development..

Emma Williams

English Education Volunteer


Emma Williams. I am an English Education Volunteer with Peace Corps Ecuador. I have a TEFL Certificate and a year and a half of practical classroom experience. I am a native English speaker, and I am actively learning Spanish. EcuaTeleTandem is a language exchange program that benefits from the interplay between native speakers and language learners. As a native speaker of English, a Spanish learner, and as a language teacher, I know that I can help facilitate meaningful conversations that lead to language acquisition on both sides. EcuaTeleTandem provides opportunities to both learn from native speakers, and to teach as a native speaker, reinforcing language acquisition for everyone involved..

Carlos Palate

Pedagogy of National and Foreign Languages


Carlos Palate holds a degree in Pedagogy of National and Foreign Languages – English from the Technical University of Cotopaxi. He has achieved the B2 CEFR endorsement of English language proficiency certified by the University of Cambridge after passing the First Certificate in English (FCE) exam. Additionally, he has earned a diploma in teacher training in English for students with special needs from the Technical University of Ambato. Furthermore, he has participated in the Introduction to the International Baccalaureate for the Middle Years Program(MYP) of the Red De Directivos De Instituciones Educativa (REDIE) and has secured the diploma of Introduction to Creativity and Innovation in Education. His research interests strongly focus on Language Education and effective classroom management strategies..
